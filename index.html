    Our Projects

    PUE Project Mpigi

    GIZ (Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit) and REA (Rural Electrification Authority) launched a tender to develop, finance, build and operate mini-grid projects in 15 villages in Rakai and Isingiro Districts in Uganda.

    PUE Project Lamwo

    GIZ and the Ministry of Energy and Mineral Development (MEMD) launched the Promotion of Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Programme (PREEEP), co-funded by EU. Through the GBE (Grüne Bürgerenergie) project in Uganda.
